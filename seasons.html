Outer Banks; Season Summary

All rights in regards to the information goes to Netflix and the show's writers.

Season 1: The Gold

Season 1 of Outer Banks follows a group of teens known as the 'Pogues' that reside in Outer Banks, NC. We get to know the group of 4, John B Routledge, JJ Maybank, Pope Heyward, and Kiara Carrera. The four of them discover John B's fathers lifelong research of the shipwreck the Royal Merchant, and they go on a quest to finish Big Johns research. In the meantime, they struggle with battles against the other side, known as the 'Kooks'. They enlist in the help of "kook princess" Sarah Cameron, whose father worked closely with John B's. They uncover long buried secrets, form relationships, and try to survive in the unreal world they call their home.

Season 2: The Cross

After the battle of the gold, and losing majorly to Ward Cameron, the teens are back to square one. The group is split up between the Bahamas and Outer Banks, and they each have battles of their own they need to get through in order to reuinte. The Royal Merchant leads them to discover the Cross of Santo Domingo, and are now tasked with trying to keep a treasure for themselves while it seems like everyone is out to get them. It seems as though they can't outrun their enemies, and one of them is always getting in trouble with the law.

Season 3: El Dorado

Not only did the teens lose the cross in an epic final battle, but they also became stranded. Their adventures this season span accross multiple countries, continents even, as they discover old family members, new enemies, and a whole new treasure. They have nothing to lose, like always. The lost city of El Dorado has a lot more to it than just being treasure, though. The enemies this time are much worse than anyone they have faced thus far. Will they finally be able to keep this treasure? Or will it to be taken from them?
